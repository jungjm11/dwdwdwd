My favorite place: Yeonhui-dong

I love Yeonhui-dong in Seoul, Korea, because my grandmother’s house was there when I was young, and I have many fond memories of the area. Although I didn’t live there while growing up, both of my parents were raised in Yeonhui-dong, which gives me a strong sense of connection and belonging. I also really enjoy the unique atmosphere of Yeonhui-dong, with its charming streets and peaceful vibe that make it such a special place to me.
